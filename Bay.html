Hanalei Bay

Hanalei bay is the largest bay on kaua'i. It spans almost two miles. I would like to visit this bay over the summer, because the waves are not too big, but they are still big enough to go boogie bording. One plus to it is because it is so large it doesn't seem too crowded.
